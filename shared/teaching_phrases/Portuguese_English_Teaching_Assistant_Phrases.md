# PORTUGUESE-ENGLISH TEACHING ASSISTANT PHRASES
## Essential Language Guide for Mathematics Education

*A practical reference for English speakers supporting Portuguese mathematics instruction*

---

## TOP 10 ESSENTIAL PHRASES FOR TEACHING ASSISTANTS

### 1. **Checking Understanding / Verificar Compreensão**

**English:** "Do you understand this concept?"
**Portuguese:** "Entendes este conceito?"
**Pronunciation:** [en-TEN-desh ESH-teh kon-SAY-too]

**Alternative:** "Faz sentido?" (Does it make sense?)
**Pronunciation:** [fash sen-TEE-doo]

---

### 2. **Offering Help / Oferecer Ajuda**

**English:** "Do you need help with this problem?"
**Portuguese:** "Precisas de ajuda com este problema?"
**Pronunciation:** [preh-SEE-zash deh ah-ZHOO-dah kom ESH-teh pro-BLEH-mah]

**Alternative:** "Posso ajudar?" (Can I help?)
**Pronunciation:** [POH-soo ah-zhoo-DAR]

---

### 3. **Encouraging Exploration / Encorajar Exploração**

**English:** "Let's try a different approach"
**Portuguese:** "Vamos tentar uma abordagem diferente"
**Pronunciation:** [VAH-moosh ten-TAR OO-mah ah-bor-DAH-zhem dee-feh-REN-teh]

**Simple version:** "Vamos tentar de outro modo" (Let's try another way)
**Pronunciation:** [VAH-moosh ten-TAR deh OH-troo MOH-doo]

---

### 4. **Visual Learning Support / Apoio Visual**

**English:** "Let me show you visually"
**Portuguese:** "Deixa-me mostrar-te visualmente"
**Pronunciation:** [DAY-shah-meh mosh-TRAR-teh vee-zoo-al-MEN-teh]

**Alternative:** "Vou desenhar para ti" (I'll draw it for you)
**Pronunciation:** [voh deh-zen-YAHR pah-rah tee]

---

### 5. **Explaining Steps / Explicar Passos**

**English:** "Let's break this into steps"
**Portuguese:** "Vamos dividir isto em passos"
**Pronunciation:** [VAH-moosh dee-vee-DEER ISH-too em PAH-soosh]

**Alternative:** "Passo a passo" (Step by step)
**Pronunciation:** [PAH-soo ah PAH-soo]

---

### 6. **Positive Reinforcement / Reforço Positivo**

**English:** "Very good! You're getting it!"
**Portuguese:** "Muito bem! Estás a perceber!"
**Pronunciation:** [MWEE-too bem! esh-TASH ah per-seh-BER]

**Alternative:** "Excelente trabalho!" (Excellent work!)
**Pronunciation:** [esh-seh-LEN-teh trah-BAH-lyoo]

---

### 7. **Redirecting Focus / Redirecionar Foco**

**English:** "Let's focus on this part first"
**Portuguese:** "Vamos focar nesta parte primeiro"
**Pronunciation:** [VAH-moosh foh-KAR NESH-tah PAR-teh pree-MAY-roo]

**Simple:** "Primeiro isto" (This first)
**Pronunciation:** [pree-MAY-roo ISH-too]

---

### 8. **Mathematical Language / Linguagem Matemática**

**English:** "This is called..." / "We call this..."
**Portuguese:** "Isto chama-se..." / "Chamamos a isto..."
**Pronunciation:** [ISH-too SHAH-mah-seh] / [shah-MAH-moosh ah ISH-too]

**Alternative:** "O nome disto é..." (The name of this is...)
**Pronunciation:** [oo NOH-meh DESH-too eh]

---

### 9. **Problem-Solving Guidance / Orientação na Resolução**

**English:** "What do you think comes next?"
**Portuguese:** "O que achas que vem a seguir?"
**Pronunciation:** [oo keh AH-shash keh vem ah seh-GEER]

**Alternative:** "E agora?" (And now?)
**Pronunciation:** [eh ah-GOH-rah]

---

### 10. **Time Management / Gestão do Tempo**

**English:** "We have a few more minutes"
**Portuguese:** "Temos mais alguns minutos"
**Pronunciation:** [TEH-moosh mah-eesh al-GOOSH mee-NOO-toosh]

**Alternative:** "Quase a acabar" (Almost finished)
**Pronunciation:** [KWAH-zeh ah ah-kah-BAR]

## PRONUNCIATION TIPS

### Key Sound Patterns:
- **ão** = [AH-oo] (like "ow" in "how")
- **nh** = [ny] (like "gn" in "lasagna")
- **lh** = [ly] (like "lli" in "million")
- **ch** = [sh] (like "sh" in "ship")
- **r** at beginning = rolled R
- **rr** = strongly rolled R

### Stress Patterns:
- Words ending in vowels: stress second-to-last syllable
- Words ending in consonants: stress last syllable
- Accents mark exceptions
