## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**2.1 Conditions for Life**
- Ex 1: Adequate distance from the Sun, adequate mass (atmosphere retention), rocky substrate, magnetic field
- Ex 2: b, d, f, c, a, e
- Ex 3: Primitive: N2 65%, O2 absent, CO2 5%, H2O 25%. Current: N2 78%, O2 21%, CO2 0.04%, H2O 0.4%
- Ex 4: Gas exchange, protection from meteors, UV protection (ozone layer), temperature moderation (greenhouse effect)
- Ex 5: a) F (the natural greenhouse effect is essential for maintaining favorable temperature), b) T, c) T, d) F (ultraviolet rays), e) T
- B1: Open answer referring to photosynthesis, CO2 reduction and O2 increase

**2.2 Cellular Organization**
- Ex 6: c, a, e, d, b
- Ex 7: a) similar organisms that produce fertile offspring; b) individuals of the same species in the same location; c) populations in the same location; d) community + habitat + relationships
- Ex 8: Biotic: living organisms (animals, plants); Abiotic: physical factors (temperature, light, water)
- Ex 9: a) T, b) A, c) T, d) A, e) T, f) A
- B2: Biomes are groups of interacting ecosystems conditioned by geography and climate

**2.3 Ecosystems**
- Ex 10: a) luciphilic, b) lucifugal, c) shade-loving, d) luciphilic, e) lucifugal
- Ex 11: Eurythermic - wide - wolf; Stenothermic - narrow - snake; Homeothermic - constant - birds; Poikilothermic - varies - fish
- Ex 12: 1-b, 2-c, 3-a
- Ex 13: a) hydrophilic, b) xerophilic, c) hygrophilic, d) mesophilic, e) xerophilic, f) hydrophilic
- Ex 14: Long shallow roots; fleshy stems to store water; leaves reduced to spines
- Ex 15: a) porosity, composition/permeability; b) seed dispersal, transport of spores/bacteria
- Ex 16: Intraspecific: same species (e.g., cooperation in meerkats); Interspecific: different species (e.g., predation)
- Ex 17: a) predation (+,-), b) commensalism (+,0), c) parasitism (+,-), d) facultative mutualism (+,+), e) amensalism (-,0)
- Ex 18: Mutualism +,+; Predation +,- (bear/salmon); Parasitism +,- (tapeworm/host); Commensalism +,0 (remora/shark); Competition -,- (lions/hyenas)
- Ex 19: a) ECTO, b) ENDO, c) ECTO, d) ENDO
- Ex 20: Camouflage: appearance similar to nature (stick insect); Mimicry: appearance similar to other organisms (false coral snake)
- Ex 21: Pollution of water/carbon cycles, deforestation, burning fossil fuels
- Ex 22: a) F (where life has never existed), b) T, c) F (where life previously existed), d) T
- B3: Open answer: without predators, prey population grows, food is depleted, many die
- B4: Open answer: lichens/mosses -> grasses/insects -> shrubs -> trees/climax community
