## Usage Notes

**Website Structure:**
- Each grade has an index page listing all topics for that year
- Each topic has a dedicated content page with detailed explanations, examples, exercises
- Content is in Portuguese, aligned with Portuguese Ministry of Education curriculum

**Topic Discovery:**
- Topic names may vary slightly across grades
- Some topics appear only in certain grades
- Natural Sciences curriculum is organized by scientific domain per grade:
  - Grade 7: Geology and Earth Sciences
  - Grade 8: Ecology and Environmental Sciences
  - Grade 9: Human Biology and Health
