## Pedagogical Rationale

This structure follows the natural progression of physical sciences:

**Physics Flow:**
1. Start with the cosmos and fundamental forces (gravity)
2. Progress to terrestrial mechanics and motion
3. Explore wave phenomena (sound and light)
4. Introduce electricity and circuits
5. Energy as unifying concept throughout

**Chemistry Flow:**
1. Begin with observable matter and lab techniques
2. Understand transformations and reactions
3. Dive deeper into atomic and molecular structure

This organization allows students to see:
- **Vertical progression:** Simple → Complex concepts within each domain
- **Horizontal connections:** Physics and chemistry as complementary perspectives
- **Unified learning:** Concepts build naturally across grade levels
