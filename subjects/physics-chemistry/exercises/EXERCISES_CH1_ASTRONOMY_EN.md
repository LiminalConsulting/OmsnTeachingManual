## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**1.1 Universe and Solar System**
- Ex 2: Planet < Planetary system < Galaxy < Group of galaxies < Supercluster of galaxies < Universe
- Ex 3: a) planet, b) planetary system, c) galaxy, d) group of galaxies, e) supercluster of galaxies
- Ex 7: a) 1.5 x 10^8 km, b) 9.5 x 10^12 km, c) 1 x 10^-9 m
- Ex 8c: 2.25 x 10^8 km (225,000,000 km)
- Ex 9: a) gaseous, b) rocky, c) gaseous, d) rocky, e) gaseous, f) rocky
- Ex 10: a) F (they are larger), b) T, c) F (shortest period), d) T, e) F (Mercury and Venus have none)

**1.2 Earth, Moon, and Forces**
- Ex 12: a) 24 hours, b) west to east
- Ex 13: a) west, b) north, c) sunrise/sunset, d) solar noon
- Ex 14: a) 365 days and 6 hours, b) 366 days, every 4 years to compensate for the extra 6h
- Ex 15: Summer solstice-B, Winter solstice-A, Autumn equinox-C, Spring equinox-D
- Ex 16: a) 27 days and 8 hours, b) rotation period = orbital period
- Ex 20: a) 4.9 N, b) 637 N, c) 11,760 N
- Ex 21: a) 784 N, b) 128 N, c) No, mass is constant
- B1: 4.028 x 10^16 m
- B2: Planet B (smaller radius = shorter distance to center = greater gravitational force)
