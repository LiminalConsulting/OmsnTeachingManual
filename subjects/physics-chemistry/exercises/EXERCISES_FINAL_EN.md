## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**8.1 Atomic Models**
- Ex 1: Dalton-B, Thomson-E, Rutherford-D, Bohr-C, Electron cloud-A

**8.2 Atoms and Elements**
- Ex 3: C (6p, 6n, 6e), Na (11p, 12n, 11e), Cl (17p, 18n, 17e), Ca (20p, 20n, 20e)
- Ex 5: Ar(Cl) = (35 x 75.8 + 37 x 24.2)/100 = 35.48
- Ex 6: Na+ (11p, 12n, 10e, +1), Cl- (17p, 18n, 18e, -1), Ca2+ (20p, 20n, 18e, +2), O2- (8p, 8n, 10e, -2)

**8.3 Electron Configurations**
- Ex 7a: i) 2, ii) 8, iii) 18; b) 8
- Ex 8: a) 1, b) 2,4, c) 2,8, d) 2,8,1, e) 2,8,7, f) 2,8,8,2
- Ex 9: d) lose 1, Na+; e) gain 1, Cl-

**8.4 Periodic Table**
- Ex 11: a) group 16, period 2; b) group 1, period 3; c) group 2, period 4; d) group 17, period 3
- Ex 13: a) 1, monopositive cations; b) 7, mononegative anions

**8.5 Chemical Bonds**
- Ex 17b: CH4, C2H6, C3H8, C4H10
- B1: Ar(Mg) = (24 x 79 + 25 x 10 + 26 x 11)/100 = 24.32
- B2: a) 1, b) lose, c) K+, d) argon (Ar)
