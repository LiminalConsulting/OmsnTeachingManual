## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**4.1 Waves and Sound**
- Ex 3: a) 50 Hz, b) 0.002 s, c) 4 m/s
- Ex 4c: 50 Hz
- Ex 5b: solids > liquids > gases
- Ex 6a: 1020 m
- Ex 7: Pitch-B, Loudness-C, Timbre-A
- Ex 8: a) 20 Hz to 20,000 Hz, c) 0 dB and 120 dB
- Ex 9d: 17 m

**4.2 Light and Optical Phenomena**
- Ex 12: Radio waves < Microwaves < Infrared < Visible light < Ultraviolet < X-rays < Gamma rays
- Ex 13a: 299,792,458 m/s (approx. 3 x 10^8 m/s)
- Ex 17d: 2 diopters
- B1: 300 m
- B3: a) diverging, b) -0.5 m (50 cm)
