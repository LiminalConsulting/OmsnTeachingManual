## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**1. Collector and Producer Societies**
- Ex 2: 1-b, 2-c, 3-e, 4-d, 5-a
- Ex 3: a) T, b) F (sources can be material or immaterial), c) T, d) F (they first appeared in Africa)
- Ex 6: Neolithic, produce, agriculture, livestock farming, sedentary, Fertile Crescent
- Ex 7: 1-b, 2-d, 3-a, 4-c

**2. First Civilisations**
- Ex 8: Egypt — Nile; Mesopotamia — Tigris and Euphrates; Indus Valley — Indus; China — Yellow River
- Ex 10: 5, 1, 3, 4, 2, 6

**3. The Greeks**
- Ex 13: Boulé — prepares draft laws / 500 members by lot; Ekklesia — approves laws / citizens; Strategoi — command army / 10 elected magistrates; Heliaia — judges cases / 6,000 jurors by lot
- Ex 14: a) F (only citizens, ~10% of population), b) T, c) T, d) F (they stayed in the *gynaeceum*, away from public life), e) T
- Ex 15: 1-b, 2-a, 3-c

**4. The Roman World and Christianity**
- Ex 16: Security, economic motivations, leaders' ambition (or social motivations)
- Ex 18: 4, 1, 5, 3, 2
