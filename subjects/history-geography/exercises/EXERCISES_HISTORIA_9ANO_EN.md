## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**1. World War I**
- Ex 2: Triple Alliance — 1882 / Germany, Austro-Hungarian Empire, Italy; Triple Entente — 1907 / England, France, Russia
- Ex 3: 1-b, 2-a, 3-c
- Ex 4: Return of Alsace-Lorraine, loss of colonies, reduction of armaments, payment of reparations (any 3)

**2. Russian Revolution**
- Ex 6: February — bourgeoisie / liberal parliamentary regime / provisional government; October — working class and peasantry / dictatorship of the proletariat / socialist government
- Ex 7: a) T, b) F (a single-party system was established), c) T, d) T, e) F (Stalin succeeded Lenin)

**3. First Republic**
- Ex 8: 5, 3, 1, 4, 2 (1891, 1908, 1910, 1911, 1926)
- Ex 9: a) E, b) L, c) E, d) L, e) E, f) L

**8. 25 April**
- Ex 15: 5, 2, 4, 1, 3
- Ex 17: President — promulgates laws / citizen voters; Government — executes laws / President chooses PM; Assembly — makes laws / citizen voters; Courts — judges / not elected
- Ex 18: a) Guinea-Bissau, b) Angola, Mozambique, São Tomé and Príncipe, Cape Verde, c) Macau (returned to China), d) East Timor
- Ex 20: 1-b, 2-a, 3-c
