## Answers

*(For verification after completing the exercises)*

**1. Location and Natural Landscape**
- Ex 1: a) F (surrounded by sea except one side – the isthmus), b) T, c) F (extreme southwest), d) T, e) F (Northern Hemisphere)
- Ex 2: a) Atlantic, b) Atlantic / Mediterranean, c) Mediterranean, d) Atlantic
- Ex 3: 1-b, 2-d, 3-e, 4-c, 5-a
- Ex 4: a) low altitude plains, b) undulating plains and low altitude plateaus, c) high altitude plateaus and mountains, d) high altitude mountains
- Ex 5: a) Westward, due to the terrain slope, b) Tagus River, c) Ebro River, d) Minho, Douro, Tagus (or others)

**2. First Human Communities**
- Ex 8: Defense against animals, warmth, cooking food
- Ex 9: a) T, b) F (became sedentary), c) T, d) T, e) T
- Ex 10: Celts, Europe, north, Iberians, south, Celtiberians, castros

**3. The Romans**
- Ex 12: 3, 1, 2, 4
- Ex 13: a) C, b) CU, c) E, d) C, e) E, f) E
- Ex 14: a) XV, b) XVI, c) VIII, d) XIII, e) XX

**4. The Muslims**
- Ex 15: a) Muhammad, b) Mecca, c) Muslims, d) Allah, e) Quran
- Ex 17: a) 711, b) Asturias and part of the Pyrenees (harsh conditions), c) Mosques/tiles, agriculture (waterwheel), words beginning with "al"

**5. Reconquest and Formation of Portugal**
- Ex 18: a) In Asturias, b) 722, Battle of Covadonga, c) Leon, Castile, Navarre, Aragon
- Ex 19: 5, 1, 4, 7, 3, 6, 2
