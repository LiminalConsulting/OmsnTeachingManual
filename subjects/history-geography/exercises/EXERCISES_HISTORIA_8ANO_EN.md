- Ex 3: 1st — Prince Henry / Rounding of Cape Bojador (Gil Eanes); 2nd — Fernão Gomes / Cape Santa Catarina; 3rd — King João II / Cape of Good Hope (Bartolomeu Dias)
- Ex 4: a) T, b) F (in the service of Castile), c) T, d) T, e) F (it was a westward detour)

**2. Renaissance**
- Ex 8: 1-b, 2-a, 3-c, 4-e, 5-d
- Ex 9: Perspective, oil painting/sfumato, naturalism and realism (or compositional balance)

**3. Reformation and Counter-Reformation**

**6. Enlightenment**
- Ex 14: 1-b, 2-c, 3-a
- Ex 15: a) E, b) S, c) C, d) E, e) C, f) S
